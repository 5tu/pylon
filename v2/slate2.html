Blowdown is a feature film by David N. Drake that Pylon is currently developing.

An explosive device planted on a multi-million dollar oil company pipeline. A group of deep sea divers are locked in a hyperbaric chamber, trying to figure out which one of them is holding the oil company for ransom.

Law of Sines is a feature film by David N. Drake that Pylon is currently developing.

It is about a young man drifting across the UK in the mid-1930s, at times a thief, a land surveyor, and a farm hand. To others he appears quiet and amenable, but inside he is fighting an ever present urge towards extreme violence.

Detachable is a BFI Network funded short film by Joshua Carver that Pylon is currently producing.

It is about a young woman who wakes up in her car after a night of heavy drinking only to discover that her hearing implant is missing. She immediately embarks on a search through her texts and photos in order to retrace her steps and find the missing device.

Small Hours is a BFI Network funded short film by Michael Mante that was produced by Pylon in June 2022.

It is about two estranged brothers that come together with an extended British-Ghanian family to remember their late father. Tensions build as the brothers are made to participate in a traditional Ghanian funeral rite, which they find hard to relate to.

Left Over is a BFI Network funded short film by David N. Drake that was produced by Pylon in September 2020.

It is about a young woman who is packing up her late father's home while simultaneously unpacking their tumultuous relationship.
